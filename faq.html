Questions, answered

Designed to remove friction before people bounce.

These accordions are ready for a Webflow CMS FAQ collection or static page build.

Do I pay during booking?

Yes for fixed packages. In production this would route to Square checkout so the booking and payment happen in one flow.

What if my scope is more complicated?

Use the custom quote form. That route is designed for larger or less standardized jobs and should create a HubSpot deal in “New Inquiry.”

How do confirmations work?

Square Appointments would handle immediate confirmation, reminders, and payment receipts by email or SMS.

Can I change my appointment later?

Yes. In production, customers would manage changes through the scheduler link in their confirmation message.
