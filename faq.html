Questions, answered

Common questions before booking or requesting a quote.

These answers help set expectations around scheduling, custom work, and follow-up.

Do I pay during booking?

This local version does not collect payment yet. It captures the request so the live payment or confirmation flow can be connected later.

What if my scope is more complicated?

Use the custom quote form. That route is better for larger jobs, multi-item repair lists, or anything that needs a more tailored estimate.

How do confirmations work?

After a request is submitted, the next step is a follow-up confirmation with timing, scope, and any needed job details.

Can I change my appointment later?

Yes. Final scheduling details can be adjusted during follow-up before the visit is locked in.
